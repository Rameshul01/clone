Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need expensive gear?
Not at all. The beginner course works with any DSLR or mirrorless camera. Even an entry-level body is perfectly fine when you learn how to use it properly.
How are the courses delivered?
All courses are video-based and on-demand. Watch at your own pace, pause, rewatch, and complete assignments whenever it suits you — lifetime access included.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If you're not satisfied within 7 days of purchase, I'll give you a full refund, no questions asked.
Do you run in-person workshops?
Yes — I run photography workshops and safaris 2-3 times a year across India. Contact me to get on the notification list.
Can I get personal photo feedback?
Course students can submit photos for critique in the community. For 1-on-1 mentoring, contact me directly to schedule a session.
Are courses available in Hindi?
Currently in English, but I publish some Hindi content on YouTube. A Hindi course version is planned for late 2025!
