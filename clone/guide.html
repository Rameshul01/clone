Camera Settings Reference

Settings Guide

Har wildlife subject ke liye best camera settings — field mein quick reference ke liye bookmark karo!

Bengal Tiger
Shutter Speed 1/800s – 1/1600s
Aperture f/4 – f/5.6
ISO 400 – 1600
Focus Mode AI Servo / AF-C
Drive Mode High Speed Burst
Metering Evaluative
Focal Length 300mm – 600mm
Pro Tip: Tiger ki aankh pe focus rakho. Jungle mein dappled light hoti hai — spot metering use karo agar background bahut bright ho.
Birds in Flight
Shutter Speed 1/2000s – 1/4000s
Aperture f/5.6 – f/8
ISO Auto (800 max)
Focus Mode AF-C + Zone AF
Drive Mode Fastest Burst
Metering Center-Weighted
Focal Length 400mm – 800mm
Pro Tip: Blue sky background pe birds shoot karo toh exposure -1 EV karo. Raptor ki aankhein sharp rakhna sabse important hai.
Elephants
Shutter Speed 1/500s – 1/1000s
Aperture f/5.6 – f/8
ISO 200 – 800
Focus Mode Single AF / AF-C
Drive Mode Single / Low Burst
Metering Evaluative
Focal Length 100mm – 400mm
Pro Tip: Elephant ke texture aur wrinkles capture karne ke liye side light best hai. Herd shot ke liye wide angle try karo.
Low Light / Dawn
Shutter Speed 1/250s – 1/500s
Aperture f/2.8 – f/4 (widest)
ISO 1600 – 6400
Focus Mode AF-C + Eye AF
Drive Mode High Burst
Metering Spot Metering
White Balance Cloudy / Manual
Pro Tip: RAW format mein shoot karo — low light noise Lightroom mein easily fix hoti hai. High ISO se mat daro!
Small Mammals & Fox
Shutter Speed 1/500s – 1/1000s
Aperture f/4 – f/6.3
ISO 400 – 1200
Focus Mode AI Servo + Small Zone
Drive Mode High Speed Burst
Focal Length 200mm – 500mm
Position Ground Level!
Pro Tip: Ground level pe lait jao — animal ki aankh ke level pe shoot karo. Yeh perspective photo ko magical banata hai!
Macro & Insects
Shutter Speed 1/200s – 1/500s
Aperture f/8 – f/16
ISO 200 – 800
Focus Mode Manual / Single AF
Drive Mode Single Shot
Lens 100mm Macro Lens
Extra Ring Flash / Diffuser
Pro Tip: Macro mein depth of field bahut kam hoti hai — f/11-16 use karo sharp results ke liye. Focus stacking try karo post-processing mein!
